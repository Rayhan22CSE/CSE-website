BSc in Computer Science & Engineering

Our undergraduate program builds a strong foundation in computer science, emphasizing both theoretical understanding and hands-on experience in software development, data structures, algorithms, and emerging technologies.

With cutting-edge labs and active student engagement, graduates are well-prepared to lead in tech industries or pursue advanced studies.

Key Courses

  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Computer Architecture
  • Operating Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Machine Learning

MSc in Computer Science & Engineering

The postgraduate program deepens knowledge in specialized areas like AI, cybersecurity, and big data. Students pursue innovative research, guided by experienced faculty and modern resources.

The curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, independent research, and professional leadership.

Specialized Courses

  • Advanced Algorithms
  • Research Methodology
  • Advanced Databases
  • Machine Learning & AI
  • Thesis / Project Work
