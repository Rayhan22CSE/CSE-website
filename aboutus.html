Notice Research Our Faculty

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Barisal is committed to delivering quality education and advancing research in computer science. Since its inception in 2014, the department has promoted innovation, critical thinking, and academic excellence.

With strong ties to industry and a curriculum designed to meet modern demands, the department equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Programs are continually updated to stay in sync with the latest technologies and best practices in the field.

Department Facilities

  • Established: 2014
  • Classrooms & Labs: 5
  • Total Faculty: 8
  • Admin & Tech Staff: 4
  • Fully-equipped computer labs with high-speed internet
  • Smart classrooms with multimedia support

Academic Programs

The department currently offers the following degree programs:

  • Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Computer Science & Engineering
  • Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science & Engineering

The department is committed to fostering a collaborative, research-driven, and student-centered environment. Our graduates are empowered to innovate, lead, and contribute meaningfully to the global tech ecosystem.
