About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Barishal is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Since its inception, the department has aimed to produce skilled graduates equipped to meet the challenges of the ever-evolving tech industry.

The department offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs that blend theoretical foundations with hands-on experience. Our dedicated faculty, modern laboratories, and dynamic academic environment foster critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning among students.
